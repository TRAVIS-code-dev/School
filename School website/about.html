About Osino Presby Senior High Technical School

Osino Presby Senior High Technical School is committed to delivering high-quality technical and general education that prepares students for tertiary study and the workplace. We focus on practical skills, strong academics, and character formation.

History

Founded to serve the Osino community, our school blends traditional values with technical training to equip students with both knowledge and hands-on skills.

Board of Governors

The School Board (governing body) provides oversight and strategic direction. Members include representatives from the Presbytery, alumni, parent-teacher association, and the local community.

  • Chair: Rev. Kwame Mensah

    Provides spiritual leadership and chairs board meetings.

  • Vice-Chair: Mrs. Akosua Agyemang

    Oversees governance and policy.

  • Alumni Representative: Mr. Joseph Adjei

    Engages former students and supports fundraising.
