Admissions

Welcome to our admissions page. Below you'll find how to apply and the requirements for entry into Osino Presby Senior High Technical School.

How to apply

  1. Complete the online sign-up form to create a student account.
  2. Submit your school leaving certificate or record of results to the admissions office.
  3. Attend the placement interview or technical assessment if requested.

Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Original or certified copies of academic records
  • Passport photo
  • Proof of age

Prefectorial Board & Student Leadership

Student leadership is an important part of our school culture. Selected prefects help run student programs, maintain discipline, and coordinate community service.

Prefects are selected based on conduct, academic standing, and leadership potential. Interested students should speak with their form teacher.
